About Jordan


I am currently a Junior at DeVry University and majoring in Computer Information Systems
with a concentration in Web Development and Administration. Professionally, I work at a
hospital in Information Systems as an HL7 Interface Engineer. I have been working in IT for
the past 5 years at Decatur Memorial Hospital. I often find myself voluntarily taking on
challenging projects and troubleshooting issues that most people try to avoid as I am always
looking for opportunities to learn and grow as much as possible. I strive to be a full stack
developer but I also excel in a team environment where I am able to communicate effectively
and efficiently with others. I am able to fit into any role requested or needed of me in a team
setting whether it be leadership, mentorship, or just simply a dependable team member that others
can trust to get their work completed in an accurate and timely manner. I personally thrive in
high pressure and high volume workloads and can keep a level head in the most stressful of times.
I try to grow and learn everyday in a all facets of life and keep an optimistic outlook with the
philosophy that anything can be turned into a learning experience and that as long as we learn
and grow from our mistakes, nothing is a failure.
